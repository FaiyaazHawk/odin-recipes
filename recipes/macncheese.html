Mac & Cheese

Description

It's my lazy man's take on the classic mac & cheese. Enjoyed at potlucks

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
  • 750 grams of sharp chedder cheese
  • 750 grams of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bag of macaroni
  • a tbsp of paprika
  • 2 tbsp of honey
  • 4 tbsp of garlic powder

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta as per instructions and set aside
  2. Pour the 2 cans of mushroom soup in a large pot
  3. Heat the soup and add in the paprika, honey and garlic powder
  4. Mix in the cheese until its fully mixed in to the soup
  5. Slowly incorporate handful of pasta bit by bit into the cheese soup mixture
  6. Once its all mixed in, pour the contents into a large pasta dish to slightly cool.
  7. Serve to your party and relax
