Mac & Cheese

Description

Its CORN with CHEESE. Its amazing. The cheese mixed with the corn with a bit of salt and pepper is wonderfully delicious. Its simple to make and is more that the sum of its parts should be.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of corn kernals
  • 500 grams of chedder cheese
  • 500 grams of mozzarella cheese
  • a shake of pepper
  • a shake of salt

Steps

  1. Saute the corn in a pan on medium heat
  2. Add salt and pepper and mix well
  3. Shred both the cheeses
  4. Add in the cheese to the pan and mix well
  5. Stir and mix well until cheese is melting and stringy
  6. Serve hot with some more mozzarella on top
